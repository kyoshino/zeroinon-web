Updating the way we work in the age of 100 years of life

The company name, coined from the English idiom "zero in on" (to concentrate, to focus), implies the meaning of "zero in on social infrastructure for the coming xR society."

Our goal is to realize a telepresence platform with a highly accessible interface that anyone can use without stress, based on devices used by many people in that era, such as PCs and smartphones now, and that provides high experience value as an xR (cross reality) social infrastructure where the real and virtual worlds merge.

Works by zeroinon

cocosoco

In May 2020, we developed a telepresence chat tool 'cocosoco' that allows users to talk using pointing icon. This improves the online videoconferencing experience at Corona Disaster.
